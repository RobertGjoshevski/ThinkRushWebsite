Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the ThinkRush app and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a password.

How do I play with friends?

You can invite friends by sharing your username or by creating a private game room with an invite code.

Is ThinkRush free to use?

Yes, ThinkRush is completely free to download and play. All core features are available without any cost.

How do I report a bug?

Use the contact form above or email us directly with details about the issue you're experiencing.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we protect your data.
